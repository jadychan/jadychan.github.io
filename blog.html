alzheimer's center

Summer 2019

Alzheimer's Family Center (AFC) in Huntington Beach is an independent nonprofit adult day health care provider dedicated to the daily care of individuals living with memory loss.


I come in once a week to perform for the New Connections Club, the center’s early-stage program. I play the piano and occasionally sing for them!


Music is very important to me and has also been researched to have great benefits for Alzheimer patients as well. Here's an excerpt from an AFC article on their website:



"A recent study conducted at the University of Miami School of Medicine revealed that music therapy helped produce more of the brain’s “feel-good chemicals” including melatonin, serotonin, and prolactin in Alzheimer’s patients. This means that after listening for a certain amount of time, the patients’ moods began improving and even facilitated some cognition. Even as cognitive functions decline, the brain still responds naturally to music, thus giving patients many benefits. Music can be used to evoke memories from a patient’s pasts. Choose your loved one’s favorite songs or musical styles and try to sing together. If your loved one is mobile, perhaps you could even try dancing to it! Not only could it bring back memories of his/her childhood, but it also may help you to connect with one another."

kode with klossy

Summer 2017-18

Kode With Klossy (KWK) is an organization that provides free 2-week coding programs for young women ages 13-18. Even without prior programming experience, scholars are able to come out of the program with the ability to create full functioning apps.

2018 instructor assistant

As an Instructor Assistant for KWK in the summer of 2018, I helped teach Ruby, HTML/CSS, and Sinatra at two Level 1 camp locations (Phoenix and Miami) where each class contained around 20 girls. I got to participate in creating new lab and lesson plan for our students to better understand the material.


Here are some of the girls' final projects!

  • Become More Eco-Friendly
  • Soul Foods in Arizona

  • Banana Central
  • Get Green Miami
